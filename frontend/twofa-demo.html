📘 HOW 2FA WORKS

Two-Factor Authentication adds an extra layer of security. After entering your email, an OTP is sent. You must enter that code to verify.

🔧 Steps:

1. Enter your email address.
2. Click "SEND OTP".
3. Check your inbox for a 6-digit code.
4. Enter the code and click "VERIFY & LOGIN".

⚠️ Educational Example

In real systems, this prevents unauthorized access even if password is stolen.
Only for educational demonstration. Use real 2FA on your accounts.
